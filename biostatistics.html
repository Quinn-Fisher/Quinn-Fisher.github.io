Biostatistics Research

Before delving into machine learning, I was quite interested in mathematical biology and public health. I was fortunate enough to research under Professor Osvaldo Espin-Garcia on a project associated with the GEM Project. The project was a multi-ancestry genome-wide association study (GWAS) via linear mixed effects model. Due to confidentiality of the project, I can't upload specific work, but below is a list of some of my responsibilities:

  • Use R and Bash scripts to execute quality control and principal component analysis on genetic data as well as prepare files for imputation.
  • Identify loci associated with faecal calprotectin, which is a sub-clinical inflammation marker.
  • Submit jobs to the Niagara cluster.
